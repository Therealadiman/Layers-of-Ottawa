How to Experience the AR

  • Open Snapchat on your mobile device.
  • Scan the provided historical figure marker image.
  • Allow camera access when prompted.
  • Move your device to explore the AR scene from different angles.
  • Listen to narration and ambient jazz music as the historical moment unfolds.
  • Continue scanning additional markers to explore new time periods.
